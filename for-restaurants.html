Common Questions

What counts as "local sourcing"?

We define local sourcing as ingredients from farms, ranches, dairies, and producers within your state or regional area. We verify your sourcing relationships during the approval process.

How do you calculate the percentage for the colored badges?

We calculate based on the number of menu categories you source locally (beef, pork, chicken, seafood, breads, produce, dairy, desserts, appetizers). Green badges = 75%+ local, Orange = 50-74%, Red = under 50%.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need your restaurant name, address, contact information, a description of your local sourcing practices, and details about which categories you source locally.

How long does the approval process take?

Most applications are reviewed within 1-2 business days. We'll contact you if we need any additional information.

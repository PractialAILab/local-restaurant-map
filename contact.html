How Can We Help?

I'm a restaurant owner. How do I get listed?

Visit our For Restaurants page to learn about our plans and submit an application. Most applications are reviewed within 1-2 business days.

How do I update my restaurant information?

Email us at bettersourceapp@gmail.com with your updated information. Include your restaurant name and what needs to be changed.

I'm a Premium member. How do I submit my monthly coupon?

Email your coupon image to bettersourceapp@gmail.com by the 10th of each month. We'll update it on the map within 24 hours.

The map isn't showing correctly. What should I do?

Try refreshing your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R). If the issue persists, email us with details about what you're experiencing and what device/browser you're using.

I found incorrect information about a restaurant. How do I report it?

Email us at bettersourceapp@gmail.com with the restaurant name and the incorrect information. We'll investigate and update it promptly.

How do I cancel my restaurant subscription?

Email us at bettersourceapp@gmail.com with your restaurant name and request to cancel. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled anytime. Annual subscriptions continue through the paid period.

I have a partnership or business inquiry.

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at bettersourceapp@gmail.com with details about your proposal or opportunity.

Response Times

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.
Restaurant applications are usually reviewed within 1-2 business days.
Technical issues are prioritized and addressed as quickly as possible.

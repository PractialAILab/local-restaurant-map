Connecting You With Local Food

We're building a community of restaurants committed to sourcing locally and supporting regional farmers, ranchers, and producers.

Our Mission

Better Source exists to make it easy for diners to find and support restaurants that prioritize local sourcing. We believe that when restaurants buy from local farms and producers, everyone wins:

  • Diners get fresher, higher-quality food
  • Local farmers and producers thrive
  • Communities grow stronger
  • The environment benefits from reduced transportation

Common Questions

What counts as "local sourcing"?

We define local sourcing as ingredients from farms, ranches, dairies, and producers within your state or regional area. We verify the restaurant's sourcing relationships during the approval process.

How do you calculate the percentage for the colored badges?

We calculate based on the number of menu categories restaurants locally source (beef, pork, chicken, seafood, breads, produce, dairy, desserts, appetizers). Green badges = 75%+ local, Orange = 50-74%, Red = under 50%.

Understanding Our Badges

Each restaurant displays a colored badge showing their local sourcing commitment

Green Badge

75%+ of menu sourced locally. Excellent commitment to local food systems.

Orange Badge

50-74% of menu sourced locally. Good commitment with room to grow.

Red Badge

Up to 49% of menu sourced locally. Making strides toward local sourcing.
